Use Case 1: Add an item to the database.

For example, assume we want to add a product "water bottles" to the database

First, we open the store app (see usecase1_a.png)

Next, we click on the "ADD A NEW PRODUCT" button (usecase1_a.png)

Next, we fill out the product information for this item, 
and click submit when finished (usecase1_b.png)

Finally, we see that our item has been added to the database (usecase1_c.png)
